Dedicated towards reaching the skies

Send2Space is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

Here in San Antonio, Texas, we strive to continue their mission of promoting educational equitability.

For the students and by the students.

LEE Send2Space is a charter of Send2Space completely run by students for students. LEE Send2Space is also a club at the International School of the Americas.

We plan to bring new STEM opportunities to our peers and ourselves by launching a high-altitude balloon into low-orbit space. Whether you've never heard of a high-altitude balloon or have sent rockets up, LEE Send2Space is here to provide new experiences, new ideas, and new colleagues.
