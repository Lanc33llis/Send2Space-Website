Dedicated towards reaching the skies

Send2Space is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

Located in San Antonio, Texas, LEE Send2Space strives to promote STEM by providing high school students with the unique opportunities that come with building a high-altitude balloon.

Built for students, by students.

LEE Send2Space is a chapter of Send2Space at LEE High School, 100% operated by students. Our main goal is to design, build, and launch a high-altitude balloon (HAB) to low-orbit space. Throughout this process, we hope to provide our members with interesting opportunities, including web development, engineering and design, and digital marketing.

Regardless of personal experience level, LEE Send2Space welcomes all students interested in any field.
